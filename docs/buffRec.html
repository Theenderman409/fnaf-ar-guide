Workshop

The workshop is where you can work woth YOUR animatronics. You can Put A CPU in them to change their Behavior. You can put a Plushsuit on them to change their appearence. You can put Remnant in them to strengthen them. And you can put MODs on them to give them abilitys.

You can have 9 endoskeletons (from what I can find), and you can send them to Salavge (find parts or a MOD), and you can send them to attack another player.
