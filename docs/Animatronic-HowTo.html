    • Info

    You start down a hallway, plushtrap on the way end. There will be a generator on either side of you, 2 meters behind you. Plushtrap will slowly move towards you, when he is in the puddle in front of you shock him. To win trhe meters must be green.

    Golden Freddy: 5

    • Info
    • Stats
    • Skins

    When you start the Golden Freddy encounter use the arrows to switch to the balloras meter move around. Otherwise switch to the mask. Check these:

    1. Is the static high Yes, do a basic Freddy encounter. No, continue to step 2

    2. If the static is low but easy to see it might be toy Freddy

    3. If the static is low and hard to see it is springtrap

    4. If the ballora meter is on it is ballora

    5. If the static is moving quickly it is mangle


    During Freddy: just pay attention to static and shock
    -No hay wires will occur

    During Toy Freddy: it is best to just keep the mask on until he charges and decloacks, if you are not fast just hold it till he charges. Follow the static shock on decloack
    - No hay wires will occur

    During springtrap: look around find some form of static without the mask if he haywires find him, White eyes = continue looking Red eyes = look away Shock on decloack
    -the eyes can change from white to red but never red to white

    During mangle: Parts will drop around the player, pick them up if he charges look at him, if he decloacks shock him

    During ballora: The noise meter will be active move slowly and keep balloras meter down by not looking at her. If the meter stays high shake the screen a little.
